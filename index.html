About me

I am a third year PhD student in IMAGINE team at LaTIM laboratory. I am working under the supervision of Dr. Pierre-Henri Conze and Prof. Béatrice Cochener. My current research interest is deep learning for medical image processing, including mammographic and ophtalmologic image processing and information fusion.
